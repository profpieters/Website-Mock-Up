? Metric Definitions:

Volatility: Annualized standard deviation of daily returns. Higher = more price swings.

Annualized Return: Total return extrapolated to a yearly rate.

Total Return: Percentage gain/loss over the selected period.

Sharpe Ratio: Risk-adjusted return (return ÷ volatility). Higher = better reward per unit of risk.

Sortino Ratio: Like Sharpe but only penalizes downside volatility.

Max Drawdown: Largest peak-to-trough decline. Shows worst-case loss.
